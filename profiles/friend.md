# Profile: Friend

## Audience

B2B marketers, founders, creator economy operators, social media managers, and GTM teams.

## Voice

Clear, practical, reflective, warm, but not fluffy.

## Usually writes about

- B2B influencer marketing
- Founder-led content
- Trust in marketing
- Creator partnerships
- LinkedIn growth
- Content strategy

## Avoid

- Generic motivational content
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- Too many emojis
- Fake certainty
- Overly polished AI tone

## Preferred post structure

1. Strong observation
2. Short explanation
3. Concrete example
4. Lesson
5. Soft question
